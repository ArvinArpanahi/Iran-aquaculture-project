# Team & Organizational Structure

## Overview

The aquaculture project is structured as a large-scale offshore fish farming operation requiring a multidisciplinary team across engineering, operations, biology, logistics, and finance.

The team is designed to ensure successful development, execution, and long-term operation of the project.

## Core Team Structure

The project is built around six key functional areas:

---

### 1. Aquaculture & Biology Team

Responsible for:

- Fish health and growth optimization  
- Stocking density planning  
- Disease prevention and control  
- Feeding strategies  

Key roles:

- Aquaculture Specialist  
- Marine Biologist  
- Fish Health Expert  

---

### 2. Engineering & Technical Team

Responsible for:

- Cage system design and deployment  
- Mooring and offshore structures  
- Feeding automation systems  
- Technical infrastructure  

Key roles:

- Marine Engineer  
- Mechanical Engineer  
- Offshore Systems Specialist  

---

### 3. Operations & Farm Management

Responsible for:

- Daily farm operations  
- Feeding execution  
- Monitoring and maintenance  
- Harvest planning  

Key roles:

- Farm Manager  
- Operations Supervisor  
- Cage Technicians  

---

### 4. Logistics & Supply Chain

Responsible for:

- Feed supply  
- Fingerling transport  
- Harvest logistics  
- Cold chain management  

Key roles:

- Logistics Manager  
- Supply Chain Coordinator  
- Export & Distribution Specialist  

---

### 5. Financial & Investment Team

Responsible for:

- Financial planning  
- Cost control  
- Investment structuring  
- Profitability monitoring  

Key roles:

- Financial Analyst  
- Investment Advisor  
- Cost Control Specialist  

---

### 6. Environmental & Compliance Team

Responsible for:

- Environmental monitoring  
- Regulatory compliance  
- Sustainability reporting  
- Risk control  

Key roles:

- Environmental Engineer  
- Compliance Officer  
- Marine Ecosystem Specialist  

---

## Organizational Model

The project follows a multi-layer structure:

- Project Developer (strategy & coordination)  
- Technical teams (design & execution)  
- Operational teams (production & maintenance)  
- Financial teams (investment & control)  
- External partners (technology & infrastructure)
