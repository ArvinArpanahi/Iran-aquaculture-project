# Technical Design

## Overview

This document defines the technical and engineering framework of the offshore aquaculture system.

The design focuses on:
- Structural stability
- Environmental compatibility
- Operational efficiency
- Scalability

---

## Cage System Specifications

### Cage Type
- Circular offshore floating cages

### Dimensions
- Diameter: 30 – 60 meters
- Depth: 10 – 25 meters

### Materials
- HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) floating rings
- Anti-corrosion steel connectors
- High-strength synthetic nets

---

## Mooring & Anchoring System

Each cage system is secured using:

- Multi-point mooring system
- Concrete or steel anchors
- Elastic mooring lines for wave absorption

Design considerations:
- Wave height resistance
- Current load handling
- Long-term durability

---

## Water Depth Requirements

### Caspian Sea:
- Optimal depth: 20 – 50 meters

### Persian Gulf & Oman Sea:
- Optimal depth: 25 – 60 meters

Reason:
- Ensures waste dispersion
- Prevents seabed pollution
- Maintains oxygen levels

---

## Environmental Considerations

The system avoids:

- Industrial discharge zones
- Urban wastewater outlets
- High sediment areas

Key environmental parameters:
- Dissolved oxygen
- Water temperature stability
- Current velocity

---

## Feeding System

- Automated feeding systems
- Centralized feed distribution
- Remote control capability

Benefits:
- Reduced feed waste
- Improved growth rates
- Lower operational cost

---

## Monitoring & Control Systems

### Sensors:
- Oxygen sensors
- Temperature sensors
- Water quality monitoring

### Surveillance:
- Cameras for security and monitoring
- Remote system access

---

## Energy System

Hybrid energy model:

- Grid electricity (if available)
- Solar panels (daytime support)
- Small wind turbines (continuous supply)

Purpose:
- Ensure 24/7 operation
- Reduce dependency on fuel

---

## Logistics & Operations

### Access:
- Service boats
- Proximity to ports

### Transport:
- Direct connection to processing facilities
- Export-ready logistics

---

## Farm Layout Engineering

- Modular grid layout
- Safe distance between cages
- Navigation corridors for boats

---

## Maintenance Strategy

- Periodic net cleaning
- Structural inspections
- Mooring system checks

---

## Conclusion

The technical design ensures:

- High production efficiency
- Long-term durability
- Environmental sustainability
- Operational feasibility
