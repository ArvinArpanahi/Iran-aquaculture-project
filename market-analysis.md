# Market Analysis

## Overview

This section analyzes the market potential for offshore aquaculture production in Iran, focusing on both domestic demand and export opportunities.

The goal is to demonstrate strong market demand, pricing stability, and long-term revenue potential.

---

## Global Aquaculture Market

Aquaculture is one of the fastest-growing food production sectors globally.

Key trends:
- Increasing global demand for seafood
- Declining wild fish stocks
- Rising consumption of protein-rich food
- Expansion of aquaculture technologies

Global demand continues to grow, creating long-term opportunities for new producers.

---

## Demand Characteristics

### 1. High Consumption

- Seafood consumption is increasing across the region
- Fish is a key protein source in many countries

### 2. Import Dependency

- Many neighboring countries rely on imports
- Limited domestic aquaculture production in some regions

### 3. Year-Round Demand

- Stable demand throughout the year
- Suitable for continuous production cycles
